Reader



What is this?

Reader is a web app that allows you to read a news article without all the bloat and clutter that many news sites have. Any article from any of the domains listed below should work.

Full List of Supported Domains

Use this site as a bookmarklet

javascript:open("https://nektro.github.io/reader/?url="+location.href,"_blank").focus()
